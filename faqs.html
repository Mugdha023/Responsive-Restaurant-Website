What types of cuisine does MN Restaurant serve?
MN Restaurant serves Asian cuisine, with a focus on Indian dishes. Our menu features a range of options for both meat and vegetarian eaters, including appetizers, dips, salads, entrees, and desserts.


Does MN Restaurant offer takeout and delivery?
Yes, MN Restaurant offers both takeout and delivery options for our customers’ convenience. To place an order for takeout, simply give us a call, and we will have your meal ready for you to pick up. If you prefer delivery, you can order through our website or third-party delivery platforms such as UberEats or SkipTheDishes.


Does MN Restaurant have vegetarian options?
Yes, we have a range of vegetarian options on our menu, including dips such as salsa, as well as entrees such as the vegetarian platter, which includes paneer, bibimbap. We also have salads and side dishes that are vegetarian-friendly.


Does MN Restaurant offer catering services?
Yes, we offer catering services for events of all sizes, including corporate events, weddings, and other special occasions. We can customize a menu to suit your needs, and our catering options include both meat and vegetarian dishes..


What are some popular menu items at MN Restaurant?
Our menu features a range of delicious options, but some of our most popular items include the Teokbokki, Biryani, Sushi, as well as the charcoal-grill meat and chicken cubes.


What are MN Restaurant's hours of operation?
We are open everyday from 11 a.m. to 9 p.m. and closed on Thursday. Hours during Ramadan 12 p.m to 10 p.m. We look forward to serving you some delicious Asian Cuisine!
